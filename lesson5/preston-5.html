Conoeing Adventure on the Bear River

Now is the time of year to enjoy the outdoors. Preston has several waterways to explore, one of which is the Bear River. Whether you are looking to explore the abundance of birds and wildlife that calls this area home, or taking in some fishing this is the place for you. Bring your family, bring your freinds, or just bring yourself for a peaceful get away from the everyday. For more information you can visit the Preston Area Chamber of Commerce at our website www.prestonchamber.org. We cannot wait to see you there.
