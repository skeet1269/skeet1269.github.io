Visit Southeast Idaho


Southeast Idaho is full of surprises. Hike to a hidden mountain lake, explore miles of hiking and biking trails, visit the famous Idaho Potato Museum and end the day with a soak in the hot pools in the city of Lava Hot Springs. Southeast Idaho is a destination for outdoor lovers and it’s no secret why. In Pocatello, home to Idaho State University, take advantage of the 50-mile City Creek Trail System for biking and hiking.

Set your adventure mode to turbo with a trip through Southeast Idaho. With off-the-beaten-path experiences at every turn, you’ll soon learn why this part of Idaho is a destination for outdoor lovers. Miles of hiking and mountain-biking trails, a hidden mountain lake and savory dishes in an unexpected setting are just a sample of the adventures that await. For more information come see us at www.visitidaho.org
