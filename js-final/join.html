Non-Profit - Reserved for Not For Profit Organizations

  • One FREE listing in the directory (print/online).

Bronze - 25 or less employees

  • One FREE listing in the directory (print/online).
  • Social Media listing.
  • Use of Chamber Conference Room.s
  • Access to all general Chamber events and programs.
  • Two FREE VIP Event Passes.
  • Promotion of Member News, Events and Discounts.

Silver - 50 or less employees

  • Same as above plus. . .
  • Three FREE listing in the directory (print/online).
  • Four FREE VIP Event Passes.
  • Excel membership database available upon request.

Gold - 50 or more employees

  • Same as above plus. . .
  • Five FREE listing in the directory including Logo (print/online).
  • Access to Six Exclusive Investor-Only Events, including VIP reception at Annual Meeting.
  • Access to key business and governmental leaders.
  • Eligible to serve on the Board of Directors and committeess.
